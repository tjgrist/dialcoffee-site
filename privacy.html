Privacy Policy

What we collect

Your email address and display name (for your account), and the content you create: reviews, brew logs, saved brew methods, and coffees you add to the catalog.

How it's used

Your email is used only for sign-in and account recovery. Your display name appears next to content you share. Brew parameters you log are aggregated (per coffee, per method) to power community brew statistics.

What's public

Reviews, brew logs, and coffees you add are visible to other Dial Coffee users under your display name. Your email is never shown to other users. Saved brew methods stay private to you.

Where it lives

Account data and shared content are stored with Supabase (our database provider). Catalog searches run against Dial Coffee's own database.

What we don't do

No advertising, no tracking SDKs, no sale of personal data, no analytics that identify you.

Your controls

Export everything you've created from Profile → Export My Data. Delete your account from Profile → Delete Account — it permanently removes your account, reviews, and brew logs from our servers.

Payments

Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple. We never see your payment details.

Contact

Questions or data requests: support@dialcoffee.site
